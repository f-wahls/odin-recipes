Macaroni and Cheese

The combination of creamy cheese, tender pasta, and the unmistakable flavor of the grill makes this dish a true game-changer. The addition of milk and eggs ensures a creamy, custard-like texture that will have you craving more.

Savor the Richness of Grilled Macaroni and Cheese!

Discover a new way to enjoy the classic mac and cheese dish! Grilling the macaroni and cheese adds a delightful crispy layer, enhancing the gooey, cheesy goodness within.

Ingredients

  • Elbow Macaroni
  • Butter or oil for saut√©ing
  • Shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • Milk
  • Eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Breadcrumbs (optional for added crunch)
  • Butter or Margarine for Grilling

Steps

  1. Boil the elbow macaroni in a pot of salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a separate pot, melt butter or heat oil. Add the shredded cheese and stir until melted.
  3. Slowly pour in the milk while continuously stirring. Let it come to a gentle simmer.
  4. Gradually add the beaten eggs to the cheese mixture, stirring constantly to prevent the eggs from scrambling. The mixture will start to thicken.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then fold in the cooked macaroni until well coated with the cheese mixture. Let it cool slightly.
  6. Butter the exterior of a sandwich or panini press. If you don't have one, you can use a skillet.
  7. Place a portion of the macaroni and cheese mixture between two slices of bread or inside a folded tortilla. Add breadcrumbs for an added crunch if desired.
  8. Grill the sandwich or tortilla wrap until the exterior is golden brown and crispy, about 2-4 minutes on each side.
  9. Remove from the heat, let it sit for a few minutes, then serve and enjoy the grilled macaroni and cheese delight!
